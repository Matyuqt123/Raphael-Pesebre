About Me

Raphael Matthew A. Pesebre, but you can call me Ram, Matyu, Sebs, or Pesebs. I’m a creative and technical writer and programmer passionate about learning new skills and contributing to projects that make a meaningful impact. My interests span across technology, literature, and the arts, and I’m always seeking ways to explore my creativity, whether that’s through crafting stories, writing code, or capturing moments in travel photography. I love to travel and experience different cultures, landscapes, and histories, turning each journey into a tapestry of memories. At the heart of my pursuits is the desire to succeed and have fun along the way, with a goal of making my mother proud and sharing my experiences with those I care about.
